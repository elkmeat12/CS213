The Desert Life

Get Involved

There are always events happening here at Desert Ridge University or even around St. George. Several musical artist come to perform and students get an extended discount on tickets.

St. George has much to offer! The Tuacahn is only 20 minutes from campus and live performances such as Tarzan, The Prince of Egypt, and many others can be viewed. Every Friday, George Fest takes place on main street. You can hear to different styles of music, buy some great tasting food, and overall have a great time taking a break from homework.

The Coyote Letter is a weekly news letter that will help you become more Involved with the community. If you are looking for an event to attend, performances to watch, job postings, or even community serve opportunities, you can find them here!
