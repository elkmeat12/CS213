Affordability
We may not have the most affordable tuition of all 4-year state institutions in Utah; however, 80% of students receive financial aid. Compared to many of the other universities in Utah, Desert Ridge has one of the highest acceptance rates at 98%.
Location & Student Life
Average temperature of 77 degrees and 300 days of sunshine year round. The beautiful Zion National Park is only 45 minutes from campus! Or see Grand Canyon National Park, just two hours away.
Academic & Learning
Desert Ridge offers 10 degrees and programs currently, but we are continually growing to provide more academic programs. With our main focus of involving students with real world applications, we seek the most exciting and interesting majors. The graduation rate at Desert Ridge is roughly 62%, with a total of 1,034 students who graduated in 2017.
