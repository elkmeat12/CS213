More About Me

I am originally from Idaho Falls, Idaho and I graduated Bonneville High School in 2014. Soon after I had graduated, I left for my mission to the Philippines. My mission was called Iloilo (elo-elo) and I learned up to 6 different dialects. The main dialect that I learned is called Hiligaynon but also know as Ilonggo. When I returned from my mission, I attended a year of school at Dixie State University but had to return to Idaho due to the expensiveness of tuition. I at least met my wife there in St. George and we were sealed in the temple December 2017. This has been my second semester at Brigham Young University - Idaho and I have enjoyed my time here.
