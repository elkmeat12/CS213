How it Works

We pick you up and you decide where! There are three places that we offer: in front of the BYU-Idaho I-Center, the Walmart in Rexburg, and right at your doorstep. All you have to do is fill out the form below to schedule a trip. Prices may vary upon distance, but typically the cost ranges from $1.50 to $2.50 for every 10 miles. You may also be interested in some of our packages, which you can view below. Payment is not requested until the day of the trip.

Each trip, we provide you with a bottle of water and your choice of snack. The van can seat up to 10 passengers and includes A/C. We play the music according to your preference, but under reasonable standards. If you schedule a day that is completely filled, we will notify you of the next available day. You will receive a text message to confirm your trip a day in advance. We are available Monday through Saturday, but are closed Sundays.

Packages:

  • Standard: Regular Costs
  • Rent the van for a day: $100
  • Date Package: Pay for 1 person, 2nd person rides for half-off
  • Veterans and Military: 10% Discount
