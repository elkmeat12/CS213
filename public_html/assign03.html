Explore Idaho

For many college students attending BYU-Idaho, they aren't very familiar with the area. Several students are either from out of state or even from another country. Being in college can get a little stressful and eventually seem boring. Sometimes students just need to take a break and work off that stress!

Here on Travel Idaho, we can get you hooked up with some of the best adventures in Idaho. You will be introduced to several of the local hot spots and we even provide shuttles! Whether you are looking for a good swim, hiking, fishing, Idaho has it all!


Here is a List of Places to Go:

  • Mesa Falls
  • Island Park
  • Lava Hot Springs
  • Swan Valley
  • Yellowstone National Park
  • Art Museum of Eastern Idaho
  • Snake River Greenbelt
  • Tautphaus Park Zoo
  • Idaho Falls Chukars Game
  • Cress Creek Nature Trail
  • Craters of the Moon
  • St. Anthony Sand Dunes
